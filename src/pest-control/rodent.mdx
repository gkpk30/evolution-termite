## Trapping, Baiting and Humane Traps

Trapping is a commonly used method for rodent removal. Our team uses humane traps to capture rodents without harming them, and we release them far away from your property to ensure that they do not return. Baiting is also an effective way to eliminate rodents, as it attracts them to poison or traps. However, it's important to use caution when using baits, as they can be harmful to pets or children if not handled properly.
